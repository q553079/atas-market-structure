# NQ Manipulation Leg Context

## Status

- `status`: `human_refined`
- `source_type`: `creator doctrine distilled into reusable context card`

## Pattern Summary

This card describes when a `manipulation leg` should be treated as a real causal move instead of a random fast bar sequence.

The leg matters when:

- it starts from a key position
- it creates meaningful displacement
- it points toward a real objective
- what happens after it confirms that the move changed inventory or expectation

The leg is not valid just because price moved quickly.

## Observed Facts To Preserve

- the leg starts from a meaningful location rather than from the middle of noise
- displacement from the origin is measurable
- a primary and secondary objective can be stated
- price behavior after the leg shows distribution, continuation, or trap release
- acceptance away from the origin is measured on follow-through

## Confirmation Signals

- the leg creates clean displacement away from the origin instead of immediate overlap
- price reaches at least the primary objective and remains capable of pressing toward the secondary objective
- the move changes what later participants must react to, rather than fading back into the origin immediately

## No-Trade Conditions

- do not label every fast impulse as a manipulation leg when it begins in the middle of balance
- do not chase a leg after most of its objective is already completed and the market is moving into exhaustion
- do not use the leg as a reason to ignore a stronger opposite higher-timeframe context

## Management Notes

- keep the leg tied to the origin and objective, not only to the shape
- if the secondary objective cannot be reached and price collapses back to origin, downgrade the read
- later analysis should distinguish between a leg that forced a new script and one that only produced temporary noise

## Review Questions

- what key position made this leg meaningful before it started
- did the move reach a real objective, or was the label applied too early
- after the leg completed, did the market distribute, continue, or fail straight back into the origin
