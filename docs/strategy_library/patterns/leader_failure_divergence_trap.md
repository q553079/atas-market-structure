# Leader Failure Divergence Trap

## Status

- `status`: `human_refined`
- `source_type`: `cross-symbol divergence doctrine synthesis`

## Pattern Summary

This pattern describes what happens when the market that seemed to be leading first loses its own hold quality, while the lagging market has already enticed followers into the same idea.

The dangerous chain is:

- leader breaks or rejects first
- lagger follows late or emotionally
- leader cannot maintain the move
- divergence appears
- the lagger's followers become trapped

This is a high-value false-opportunity filter because many bad trades come from assuming a lagging market is confirmed when the leader has already started failing.

## Observed Facts To Preserve

- which market led first
- whether the lagging market followed before or after the leader had real acceptance
- where the leader first lost hold quality
- whether divergence appeared while the lagger was still extended
- whether trapped followers in the lagging market were then forced to exit

## Confirmation Signals

- the leader first appears valid, then loses hold quality or fails its break
- the lagging market continues to price the old story longer than the leader
- once divergence becomes obvious, the lagging market reverses or releases trapped followers

## No-Trade Conditions

- do not keep trading the lagging market in the old direction once the leader has clearly failed
- do not call divergence actionable before the leader's failure is actually visible
- do not fade the lagger too early if the leader has not yet lost its hold quality

## Management Notes

- this card is strongest after a prior leader already signaled the move and then failed
- the best opportunity is often after the lagger proves it was the late market, not at the first tiny divergence
- once the lagger fully resets and synchronizes again, the trap edge is gone

## Review Questions

- did the leader really fail, or was the read just early
- was the lagging market still being traded as if confirmation remained valid
- when divergence appeared, did the operator react to the leader failure or cling to the late follow-through story
