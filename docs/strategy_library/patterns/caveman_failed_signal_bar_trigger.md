# Caveman Failed Signal Bar Trigger

## Status

- `status`: `human_refined`
- `source_type`: `price-action failed-break scalp doctrine synthesis`

## Pattern Summary

This pattern formalizes the Caveman setup as an immediate failure of a strong signal bar by an even stronger opposite trigger bar.

The business edge is not "two bars reversed."
The business edge is:

- one side committed hard
- that commitment failed immediately
- trapped followers now need out quickly

This is an aggressive scalp-reversal doctrine, not a generic reversal doctrine.

## Observed Facts To Preserve

- how strong the original signal bar actually was
- whether the signal bar represented a real breakout or continuation attempt
- whether the opposite trigger bar truly negated the signal bar instead of only opposing it
- whether the trigger closed through the signal bar open or extreme
- whether a retest of the failed breakout point offered a cleaner entry
- whether there was enough room for a scalp target before major opposing structure

## Confirmation Signals

- the signal bar is large, directional, and closes near its extreme
- the trigger bar closes far enough through the signal bar to prove failure, not hesitation
- the original direction cannot immediately reassert itself on the first retest

## No-Trade Conditions

- do not use this pattern if the signal bar itself is weak
- do not use it in overlapping chop or auction middle
- do not use it if the trigger only partially negates the signal bar
- do not use it if there is no target room for a scalp before major opposing structure

## Management Notes

- this setup is strongest when follow-through appears quickly
- a retest of the failed breakout point can be cleaner than chasing the trigger close
- if the opposite side cannot extend or hold quickly, the edge decays fast

## Review Questions

- was the signal bar strong enough to trap real followers
- did the trigger truly negate the signal bar or only pause it
- was the trade taken at a meaningful edge or just inside noise
- did the operator treat a scalp-reversal setup like a guaranteed swing reversal
